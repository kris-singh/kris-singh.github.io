Krishnakant (Krish) Singh

I am a PhD student at the Visual Inference Lab at TU Darmstadt, advised by Prof. Stefan Roth. My research focuses on computer vision and machine learning. I also work in close collaboration with Prof. Simone Schaub-Meyer.

I am interested in generative modeling with a particular focus on compositionality and disentanglement of scenes. I previously spent three beautiful years at Rakuten research working on object detection, retrieval, and complementary item recommendation for fashion images. I completed my graduation from IIT Hyderabad , where I was advised by Prof. Vineeth N Balasubramanian .
